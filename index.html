50 minute full training sessions and 30 minute express sessions are offered. Nutrition coaching packages with biweekly check-ins for accountability and support are also available!

NUTRITION COACHING


Whatever your fitness goals, proper nutrition is necessary to reach them. Proper nutrition increases energy, enhances mood, improves memory, and helps you get a better night’s sleep!

Nutrition coaching includes a personal calculation of macronutrients (protein, carbs, and fats) that you should consume each day. You will log your food each day and I will check your diary twice weekly and provide feedback. Once a week, we will have a check-in call to review the previous week and plan for the next week.

There are no forbidden foods, and you can still enjoy the food you love while reaching your goals!

DNA-BASED TRAINING


DNA based training is a popular trend in the fitness world today. With this program you will have your DNA tested and analyzed to determine which specific type of training program is best suited for you.

Ever wondered how efficiently your body burns fat during cardio or how it utilizes protein? Then this custom program is for you!
